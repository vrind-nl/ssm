About

Solo Sailor's Mate (SSM) is a simple tool to help a solo sailor.

It has two basic functions:

  1. The Trace function traces your historic positions, including distance, speed over ground, and course.
  2. The Trip function traces your progress over 6 hour intervals. You can record this information in your log.
  3. The Watch function plays an alarm at set intervals to wake you up between naps.

SSM can be useful:

  • if the solo sailor cannot afford expensive electronics (SSM works on any phone with GPS), or
  • as a backup when said electronics fail.

You can install SSM on your phone, so you can use it even when your off-line.

SSM is designed to have minimal dependencies:

  • It uses Geodesy for geodesic calculations.
